Why Donate to Berry Ultimate?

Donating any amount to Berry Ultimate helps sustain our club by covering tournament bid fees, travel expenses, and ensuring Ultimate remains accessible to all students who want to play at Berry.

Berry Bucks Leadership is currently working on setting up a method for donations.

Thank you for your patience and please reach out to BerryBucksUltimate@gmail.com if you feel led to give at this moment!
